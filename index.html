Password Strength Checker

A front-end tool to evaluate password security in real-time. No data is stored or sent anywhere.

Strength: Weak

How Strength is Calculated

  • Length: At least 12 characters (bonus for 16+).
  • Variety: Uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols.
  • Avoids common patterns (e.g., 'password', sequential keys).
  • Score: 0-100 (Weak <40, Medium 40-70, Strong >70).
